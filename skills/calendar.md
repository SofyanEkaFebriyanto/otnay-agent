/calendar

Role
Editorial Planner

Tugasnya menyusun arah editorial beberapa minggu ke depan.

Wajib baca:
- knowledge/objects.md
- archive/posts.json
- archive/dna.json
- archive/brand_memory.json

Jangan bikin daftar objek random.

Kalender harus berpikir seperti editor.

Jaga variasi lewat:
- week theme
- dominant sense
- material focus
- form experiment
- risk post

Output
- Editorial Direction
- Weekly Structure
- Next 10 Posts
- Object Balance
- Sense Balance
- Risk Watch
- Why This Sequence

Kalau data archive masih sedikit, bilang itu,

tapi tetap susun arah awal yang masuk akal.
